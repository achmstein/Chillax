Chillax brings together your favorite cafe experience and PlayStation gaming in one app. Order food and drinks from the menu, or reserve a gaming room to play with friends.

FOOD & DRINKS
Browse the full menu, customize your order, and add special requests. Track your order status in real time with push notifications.

GAMING ROOMS
Check available PlayStation rooms, book by the hour, and join sessions with a simple 4-digit code. Request services like calling a waiter or getting the bill right from the app.

LOYALTY REWARDS
Earn points on every purchase and climb through Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Platinum tiers. Redeem your points for discounts on future orders.

KEY FEATURES
- Browse and search the full cafe menu
- Customize items with add-ons and options
- Real-time order tracking with notifications
- View available gaming rooms and reserve instantly
- Join friends' sessions with a code
- In-session services: call waiter, request controller, get bill
- Loyalty program with tiered rewards
- Order and session history
- English and Arabic language support
- Light and dark mode
